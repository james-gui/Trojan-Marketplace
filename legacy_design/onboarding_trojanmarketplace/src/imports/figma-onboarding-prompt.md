**Prompt for Figma AI:**

Design a smooth, modern onboarding flow for a student-only marketplace app called **Trojan Marketplace**, built exclusively for USC students to buy and sell services.

The app already has:

* A landing page
* A dashboard with two tabs:

  * “Browse Services”
  * “My Requests”

Create the onboarding flow that happens immediately after account creation.

Style:

* Clean, modern, mobile-first
* USC-inspired color palette (cardinal red + gold accents, subtle not overwhelming)
* Friendly, energetic, student-focused tone
* Strong trust signals and community feel
* Rounded cards, soft shadows, simple UI

---

### Screen 1: Welcome / Intent Selection

Headline:
“Welcome to Trojan Marketplace 🏛️”
Subtext:
“Built exclusively for USC students to buy and sell services.”

Primary buttons (large, stacked):

* Find a Service
* Offer a Service
* Do Both

Include subtle USC identity elements (like “Fight On” energy, but professional).

---

### Screen 2: Quick Profile Setup

Goal: Lightweight and fast (under 60 seconds)

Fields:

* Profile photo upload (optional but encouraged)
* Major (dropdown)
* Graduation Year (dropdown)
* Campus affiliation (Marshall, Viterbi, Dornsife, Athletics, Clubs, etc.)
* Short bio (1 sentence max)

Include trust indicator text:
“Only visible to verified USC students”

Add a progress bar at the top (Step 2 of 4).

Primary CTA: Continue

---

### Branch Flow A: If User Selects “Find a Service”

Screen 3A: Category Selection

Allow user to select 3–5 interests:

* Tutoring
* Resume Help
* Graphic Design
* Video Editing
* Photography
* Moving Help
* Fitness Coaching
* Tech Help
* Study Guides
* Event Staffing

UI:
Selectable rounded category cards with icons.

Primary CTA:
“Show Me Services”

---

Screen 4A: Personalized Feed Preview

Show curated listings in card format:
Each card includes:

* Profile photo
* Name + Major + Graduation Year
* Service title
* Starting price
* Rating (placeholder)
* Message button

Encourage first action:
Banner at top:
“Message your first Trojan today.”

---

### Branch Flow B: If User Selects “Offer a Service”

Screen 3B: Service Creation (Multi-step with progress bar)

Step 1:
“What are you offering?”

* Service title field
* Category dropdown

Step 2:
Description field with prompts:

* Who is this for?
* What’s included?
* Why choose you?

Step 3:
Pricing model:

* Hourly
* Fixed price
* Starting at $

Step 4:
Availability selector

Step 5:
Upload sample work (optional)

Each step should feel lightweight and minimal.

Final Screen:
“🎉 Your service is live!”
Buttons:

* Browse students looking for services
* Share your listing

---

### Global UX Elements to Include

* Toggle at top of dashboard:
  “You are browsing as: Buyer / Seller”
* USC email verified badge
* “Verified Trojan” badge
* Clean navigation
* Minimal friction
* 2–3 minute total onboarding time

Design this as a complete clickable mobile onboarding flow with clean spacing, strong hierarchy, and a Gen Z but professional aesthetic.
